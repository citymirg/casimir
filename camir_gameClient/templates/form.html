Spot the Odd Song Out

Collecting Similarity Data via the CaSimIR API

Spot the Odd Song Out is part of a research project at City University London. We are interested in the way you compare music, and how that relates to your musical and cultural background.

Following termination of your usage of Spot the Odd Song Out, we may retain and continue to use your non-personal information in de-identified form for purposes of research: we use your game-usage and non-personal information to create a freely available dataset intended for use in music similarity research, undertaking great care to remove any personally identifiable information.

Please note that when you continue to the game you agree to our Privacy Policy
